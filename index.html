    EL-citador Foundation Goals

    Our goals and missions

    El-citador Foundation International is out on a mission to help orphans, disabled and less priviledged children by provision of the below Amenities:

    Building of Schools

    We are out to provide housing and shelter to homeless children, as well as renovation and building of school buildings in rural places for poor and needy children to attend.

    Medical facilities

    We also provide medical and health equipments to orphanage homes and the less priviledged Parents for their wards and kids.

    Food & Relief items

    Since food and water are integral parts of human existence, We strive to make sure that needy get that too. We also provide othjer relief items.

    Education facilities

    Education as a key to change the world has prompted us to focus our attention on any and everything that pertains to educating the orphans and helpless children.

    How we work

    HOW EL-CITADOR FOUNDATION OPERATES

    • 01Raise money from different sources.

    • 02Seperate all the Donations collected for each Donation Cause.

    • 03Set up a Committee of volunteers for the disbursement of the funds and relief materials for each Donation Cause.
