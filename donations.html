Education For Poor and Less Priviledged Children

 

Owing to the high rate of illiteracy in Africa and other developing Countries millions of Children drop out of School Yearly.

To this effect, We seek to help this poor and unpriviledged Children by Providing them With Basic Education Opportunities through Sponsorship and Awarding of Scholarships to deserving Children.

Provision of Shelter and Food for Internally Displaced Persons

 

Over the Years, the Advent of War, Pandemic and Natural disasters has increased the number of Homeless Children and Adults, Thus giving room for relief funds for provision of shelter and rehabilitation for internally displaced Persons.

To this end, We've decided to support this Persons by Providing Shelter, Clothing and Food. As well as rehabilitation of wounded and disabled persons.

How we work

HOW EL-CITADOR FOUNDATION OPERATES

  • 01Raise money from different sources.

  • 02Seperate all the Donations collected for each Donation Cause.

  • 03Set up a Committee of volunteers for the disbursement of the funds and relief materials for each Donation Cause.

  • 04Disburse the Funds gathered for each Cause to the right set of People.
